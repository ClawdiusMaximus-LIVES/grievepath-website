Features

nothing you don't

Built for clarity in chaos. Every feature exists to lift one more weight off your shoulders.

Smart Task Checklist

Deadline Reminders

Important deadlines are calculated from the date of passing. Gentle notifications ensure nothing time-sensitive slips through when your mind is elsewhere.
