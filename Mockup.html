This is one of the first and most important steps. It can feel overwhelming, but having these copies will make every other task easier. You're doing the right thing.

What to do

Request multiple certified copies from the county vital records office. You'll need these for nearly every financial and legal task — banks, insurance companies, the court, and government agencies will all ask for one. Ask for at least 10 copies.
